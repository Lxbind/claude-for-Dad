Quick check before we begin — what's the goal of this course?

  • Learn how AI works under the hood
  • Figure out where AI helps in my actual procurement work
  • Replace parts of my job with AI
  • Read theory about large language models

How it works

  • Eight lessons, 15–30 minutes each. One per sitting. No homework in between.
  • Pick-and-learn. Multiple choice with feedback on every idea. You pick, see why, move on.
  • Your work is the textbook. Lesson 1 is you filling in your actual Sanofi tasks. Every lesson after that references that chart.
  • No installs through Lesson 5. Lesson 6 opens claude.ai (free signup). Lesson 7 introduces Claude Code.
